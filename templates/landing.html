How AttendEase Works

1. Teacher Login

Teachers securely log in to create and manage attendance sessions via a simple dashboard.

2. QR Code Generation

For each lecture, a unique QR code is generated and displayed for students to scan.

3. Student Check-In

Students scan the QR using Google Lens or any QR app. On first scan, they enter their name & roll number.

4. Device Mapping

Student devices are auto-mapped after first check-in — no repeated data entry needed.

5. QR Expiry Control

Teachers can stop the QR after a set time to prevent late or fake entries.

6. Attendance Reports

Teachers can view lecture-wise attendance reports directly from the dashboard.
